Aimee Miller

My ambition is to deliver high quality solutions that achieve business objectives without compromising user experience. I have spent much of my career working in small multi-disciplinary teams in the Australian Public Service. I am proud to work on complex problems and deliver solutions that truly impact the lives of our users.

I am experienced in service, UX & visual design as well as user research and project coordination. I like working on projects that create positive change in peoples lives. I love the challenge of working within business constrains to improve existing user journeys.
